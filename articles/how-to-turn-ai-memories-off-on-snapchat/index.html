Understanding Memories Settings

Under “Privacy Controls,” find “Memories.” Here you can choose where your snaps are saved:

  • Memories & Cloud: Saves snaps to both your device and Snapchat’s cloud.
  • Memories Only: Saves snaps only to Snapchat’s cloud.

Frequently Asked Questions About Snapchat AI and Memories

Can I prevent Snapchat from saving my snaps to Memories?

Yes, you can prevent Snapchat from automatically saving your snaps to Memories. Go to Settings > Privacy Controls > Memories, and select “Do Not Save.” This ensures snaps are only saved to your device’s camera roll or not saved at all after sending or viewing. This is a direct method for managing data saving related to how to turn AI memories off on Snapchat.

Does Snapchat use AI to read my chats?

Snapchat’s primary design emphasizes ephemeral messaging, meaning chats are typically deleted after viewing or after they expire. While AI might be used for content moderation or to improve features like search within chats, the platform does not claim to use AI to “read” your chats for purposes of building a persistent conversational memory of your interactions in the way an AI assistant would. Always refer to their official privacy policy for the most accurate information.

How does Snapchat use AI for features other than Memories?

Snapchat employs AI in various features, including Lens filters, friend suggestions, content recommendations on the Discover page, and for detecting policy-violating content.
